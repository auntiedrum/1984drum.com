Swap or Burn

Nothing is permanent in life.
This collection reminds us how important it is to appreciate the present, and how beauty and life are temporary and transitory.
When any NFT in this collection is purchased, a timer counts down. During the countdown, its owner can swap the digital NFT back to 1984drum in return for the physical artwork used to make the piece.
Physical work is sent by special delivery, with a hidden message on the back, used as part of the proof of delivery. 1984drum will then burn the digital NFT.
If the NFT is not swapped, a video will be made of the artwork being erased off the paper, and then burned, with fire, to the point of complete destruction. The destruction video will be posted on the 1984drum social accounts and minted as a completely separate NFT.
